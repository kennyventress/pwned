Purpose

Whether you are looking to try something new or are an experienced bartendar, we can all agree that looking for online beverage recipes is always such a hassle. For this reason, we created an app containing over 100 recipes for alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages that can be accessed in a few simple steps.

What are our Goals?

Our goal was to create a user friendly app that provided numerous of beverage options to choose from. Beverages can be searched by name, first letter and/or ingredients.
